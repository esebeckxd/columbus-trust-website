About

For more than 25 years, we have navigated insurers and institutional investors through the complex currents of Europe's financial markets.

Since 1999, Columbus Trust has supported its clients as a strategic partner and transaction advisor — with a particular focus on the financial services sector and the German-speaking insurance market.

Across numerous mandates we have helped corporates and investors design resilient strategies for the low-interest-rate environment, master the demands of digital transformation, and execute complex transactions successfully.

Our strength lies in the combination of deep industry knowledge, a personal international network, and a consistent orientation to the regulatory, supervisory and tax frameworks shaping our clients' decisions.

Services

Two disciplines.
One uncompromising standard.

Our offering combines strategic advisory for the insurance industry with bespoke transaction support across alternative asset classes — institutional depth, delivered as a partnership.

01 — Transaction Advisory

Private Market Secondary Transactions

We support German-speaking institutional investors in shaping and executing secondary transactions in private markets — a growing, highly dynamic arena that rewards experience and structure.

  • Assessment of strategic rationale and value-realisation options
  • Design of optimal deal and sale structures
  • Efficient execution of structured bidding processes
  • Access to an international circle of credible buyers
02 — Strategic Advisory

Financial Services Advisory

We advise insurance companies on the defining challenges of the prolonged low-interest-rate environment, digital transformation and portfolio / run-off transactions — with particular depth in continental Europe.

  • Development of sustainable strategies for life insurers
  • Process standardisation, efficiency & automation
  • Evaluation and execution of digital transformation
  • M&A advisory across DACH and continental Europe
  • Run-off transactions in the life insurance market
